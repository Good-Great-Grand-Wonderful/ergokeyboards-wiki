# Welcome to the Crosses V2 Docs

![The Crosses V2 split keyboard](/img/crosses-v2-hero.png)

This site covers everything you need to build and customize your Crosses V2 split keyboard, powered by [ZMK firmware](https://zmk.dev/).

The Crosses V2 comes in two variants (**Crosses** and **Bridges**) and three sizes: 36, 42, and 54 keys. Everything here applies to both variants; the few places where they differ are called out along the way.

Need help? [Join the Discord!](https://discord.gg/uQb9UgrGbe)

## Where to start

**Building from a kit?** Follow the build guides in order:

1. [Soldering](/crosses-v2/build-guide/soldering) - solder a few connectors, and hotswap sockets
2. [Final Assembly](/crosses-v2/build-guide/final-assembly) - install heat-set inserts, route cables, and close up the case

Then [flash the firmware](/crosses-v2/firmware/flashing) and [connect](/crosses-v2/firmware/connecting).

**Purchased a build?** Your keyboard comes loaded and tested out of the box, so skip straight to [connecting it](/crosses-v2/firmware/connecting).

**Making it yours?** [Tune your trackballs](/crosses-v2/trackball-tuning) from the browser, or [customize your keymap](/crosses-v2/firmware/customizing) with ZMK Studio or your own firmware.
