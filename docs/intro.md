# Welcome to the Crosses V2 Docs

![The Crosses V2 split keyboard](/img/crosses-v2-hero.png)

This site covers everything you need to build and customize your Crosses V2 split keyboard, powered by [ZMK firmware](https://zmk.dev/).

The Crosses V2 comes in two variants — **Crosses** and **Bridges** — and three sizes: 36, 42, and 54 keys. Everything here applies to both variants; the few places where they differ are called out along the way.

Need help? [Join the Discord!](https://discord.gg/uQb9UgrGbe)

## Where to start

**Building from a kit?** Follow the build guides in order:

1. [Soldering](/build-guide/soldering) — solder a few connectors, and hotswap sockets
2. [Final Assembly](/build-guide/final-assembly) — install heat-set inserts, route cables, and close up the case

Then [flash the firmware](/firmware/flashing) and [connect](/firmware/connecting).

**Purchased a build?** Your keyboard comes loaded and tested out of the box — skip straight to [connecting it](/firmware/connecting).

**Making it yours?** [Tune your trackballs](/trackball-tuning) from the browser, or [customize your keymap](/firmware/customizing) with ZMK Studio or your own firmware.
